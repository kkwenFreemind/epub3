
# 祝福 / Blessings

願這個世界充滿愛與和平，願每一個生命都能找到內心的寧靜與喜悅。  
*May this world be filled with love and peace, may every life find inner tranquility and joy.*

願所有的善知識都能指引我們走向光明，願每一個需要祝福的生命都能感受到溫暖與希望。  
*May all wise teachers guide us towards the light, may every life in need of blessings feel warmth and hope.*

願我們在閱讀這些智慧之書時，能夠獲得啟發，培養慈悲心，貢獻社會，造福眾生。  
*May we gain inspiration while reading these books of wisdom, cultivate compassion, contribute to society, and benefit all beings.*

願世界和平，人民安樂，萬物和諧。  
*May the world be peaceful, people be happy, and all things be harmonious.*

此倉庫收集了各種善知識書籍，希望能為有緣人帶來益處。  
*This repository collects various books of wise teachings, hoping to bring benefits to those who are destined.*
